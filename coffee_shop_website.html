Our Story

Born from a love of the slow cup

Ember & Ground started in a converted garage in 2019, when co-founders Mia and Daniel decided the city needed a coffee shop that treated every bean as an ingredient, not a commodity.

We work directly with smallholder farmers across Ethiopia, Colombia, Kenya, and Guatemala — building relationships that go beyond the transaction. When you drink with us, you're connected to the hands that grew your coffee.

Our roastery is two blocks away. Every batch is roasted within 72 hours of your order. No stale shelves. Just fresh, living coffee.
